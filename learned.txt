what is a tiger|A tiger is a large carnivorous mammal that belongs to the Felidae family. There are six subspecies of tiger, which are:

1. Bengal tiger (Panthera tigris tigris)
2. Indochinese tiger (Panthera tigris corbetti)
3. Malayan tiger (Panthera tigris jacksoni)
4. Sumatran tiger (Panthera tigris sumatrae)
5. Siberian tiger (Panthera tigris altaica)
6. South China tiger (Panthera tigris amoyensis)

Tigers are native to Asia and are found in a variety of habitats, including forests, grasslands, and mangrove swamps. They are apex predators and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems.
